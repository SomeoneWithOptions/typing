# Typing

A simple and smart way to master touch typing. This app helps you build muscle memory by focusing on what you need most, using real words and adaptive lessons.

## How it works

- **Start Simple**: You'll begin with just a few common letters (`e n i a r l`). As you get faster and more accurate, new letters will automatically unlock until you've mastered the entire alphabet.
- **Learn with Real Words**: No more typing random strings of characters. All practice sessions use real English words to help you learn natural typing patterns.
- **Adaptive Practice**: The app tracks which keys you find difficult and automatically builds lessons to help you improve where it counts.
- **Focus Mode**: Want to drill a specific key? Switch to Focus Mode to target any letter of your choice.
- **Track Your Progress**: See your speed (WPM) and accuracy for every letter. A visual keyboard map helps you keep your eyes on the screen instead of your hands.

## Good to know

- **Private & Local**: Your progress is saved directly in your web browser. There are no accounts to create and your data never leaves your device.
- **Offline Ready**: Once loaded, you can keep practicing even without an internet connection.
- **Desktop First**: This experience is designed for use with a physical QWERTY keyboard.

## Getting started

Just open the app and start typing the words shown on the screen. The app will guide you through the rest!
